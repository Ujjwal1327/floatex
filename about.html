Our Projects
Our Story

Floatex Solar helps project developers unlock clean energy from India's vast network of water bodies through precision-engineered floating solar systems.

Blending deep engineering expertise with sustainable innovation, we design systems that reduce land use, minimise evaporation, and maximise energy yield — a triple win for the environment and the grid.

By combining rigorous project management with world-class materials, we've built a track record of reliable floating solar installations across reservoirs, canals, and industrial ponds. Today, our mission goes beyond megawatts — we're building infrastructure that reshapes how India powers its future.

Our Process

How Floatex Solar Works

1

Site Assessment

We survey the water body — measuring depth, surface area, solar irradiance, and grid proximity — to determine the optimal system size and layout for maximum yield.

2

Engineering & Design

Our engineers design a custom floating structure, anchoring system, and electrical layout tailored to the site — balancing structural integrity, wave resistance, and energy efficiency.

3

Installation & Commissioning

Our experienced crew deploys the floats, mounts the panels, and connects the system to the grid — delivering a fully commissioned, operational floating solar plant.

Since 2017

Company History

Founded with a singular vision — to turn India's vast water bodies into engines of clean energy — Floatex Solar began operations with a small but determined team of engineers and energy experts.

Over the years, we expanded our capabilities across project development, structural engineering, and grid integration. From our first pilot installation to commissioning 15+ floating solar plants across India's major reservoirs, every milestone has been built on technical precision and an unwavering commitment to sustainability.

Today, Floatex Solar stands as a trusted name in India's renewable energy landscape — partnering with government bodies, IPPs, and industrial clients to deliver floating solar solutions that are reliable, scalable, and built for the long term.
