Our work process

Floatex Solar unlock the potential of our energies for a future better

Our aspiration is to be recognised globally as a company of excellence, high growth, and superior returns, with an entrepreneurial team and a pioneering approach to energy evolution.

Our Floatex Way identifies the values that define how we operate to achieve this.

1. Site Evaluation and Assessment

We assess water conditions, usable area, depth, and environmental factors while planning logistics and installation feasibility.

2. System Design and Optimization

We design efficient floating layouts with optimized anchoring, mooring, and integrated electrical and mechanical systems.
