About This Project

The Simhadri floating solar project represents a collaboration between Floatex Solar and BHEL for NTPC's reservoir in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. This facility features six blocks of varying sizes — an engineering choice that optimises energy capture and adapts to the reservoir's irregular geometry.

Pile anchoring technology secures the installation against the hydrological conditions of the Simhadri reservoir. Using 330 Wp solar panels, the facility generates clean energy while setting a global benchmark for eco-friendly, water-based power generation. It demonstrates how existing thermal power plant reservoirs can be repurposed for renewable generation alongside their primary function.
