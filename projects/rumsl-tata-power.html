About This Project

Unit E of the Omkareshwar Floating Solar Park sees Floatex Solar partnering with TATA Power to deliver 126 MW of clean energy from the Omkareshwar reservoir. Comprising 20 floating sections of 5 MW AC each, this unit advances India's most ambitious floating solar project towards its 600 MW target.

Using 545 Wp solar panels with Deadblock anchoring technology, the installation is engineered for the monsoon-affected reservoir conditions of Madhya Pradesh. RUMSL integrates multiple units from different contractors to deliver this world-record floating solar park. Omkareshwar is set to become a global case study in utility-scale FSPV and India's clean energy ambition.
