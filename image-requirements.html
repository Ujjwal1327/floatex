FLOATEX SOLAR — IMAGE REQUIREMENTS

Comprehensive Media Guide for Graphic Designers

📁 Formats & Quality

  • Primary Format: WebP (lossy, 80% quality).
  • Fallback: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics.
  • Keep file sizes under 200KB where possible.

📐 Aspect Ratios

  • Standard Desktop: 16:9
  • Standard Mobile: 9:16 (Vertical) or 4:3
  • Service Items: 1:1 (Perfect Square)

💡 Pro-Tip for GD

  • Do NOT put text on images. (Text is done in code).
  • Ensure important subjects are Centered for cropping.
  • Match names to the Section Name in the table.
